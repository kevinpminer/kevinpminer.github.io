CV

The arenas of business, politics, and society are becoming increasingly coupled. Their recursive interactions will define the next generation of large-scale organizations and democratic institutions.

I am a researcher applying theory from organizational sociology and strategy, alongside rigorous qualitative and interpretive methods, to bring analytical clarity to this transformation.

I collaborate directly with executives and senior leaders at Fortune 500 firms, global consulting organizations, the United Nations, and major philanthropic organizations, as well as leading business and political scholars, to analyze the decisions shaping the evolving relationship between firms and the societies in which they operate.

I am affiliated with the Oxford Centre for Corporate Reputation and the Skoll Centre for Social Entrepreneurship.
