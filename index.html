Welcome! I am a DPhil candidate at Saïd Business School, University of Oxford, and currently a visiting researcher at the Questrom School of Business, Boston University. My research sits at the intersection of organizational sociology, institutional theory, and strategy. I use qualitative and interpretive methods to study how organizations navigate social and political pressures—and how ideas travel, transform, and endure within and across organizational settings.

I am affiliated with the Oxford Centre for Corporate Reputation and the Skoll Centre for Social Entrepreneurship. Prior to Oxford, I worked as a Business Analyst at McKinsey & Company.
